Our Home

It was a dream to open an authentic Italian Pizza joint with exceptional quality and splendid customer service for our Coimbatore residents for a long time. It has come into fruition when the Co-founder who himself is a qualified Chef worked nearly two decades in varied five-star hotels internationally such as Marriott, Jumeirah, & Kempinski, in the Middle East, hotels in India as well as in Europe (Luxury cruise ship), opened his primary Pizza Joint in Ramanathapurm, Kovai. He has acquired different awards and laurels for his supreme cooking skills and mouth-watering culinary dishes. Jonats products are prepared with superior hygiene, quality ingredients like freshly rolled dough from the scratch using genuine mozzarella cheese that is lavishly topped on each pizza accompanied by tender meat and garden-fresh vegetables for the utmost satisfaction 0f our guests. Our motto is to serve authentic Italian pizzas and savories to our guests with absolute fulfillment.
